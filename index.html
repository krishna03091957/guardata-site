Why DPDP Compliance Matters

⚠️ Mandatory from May 2027

Every business processing personal data of Indian citizens must comply with DPDP. Penalties up to ₹250 crore for violations.

🚀 No Size Exemptions

DPDP applies to startups, SMEs, and enterprises equally. Even a 2-person team collecting emails must comply.

✅ Affordable Solutions

Basic compliance costs ₹5,000-₹20,000. You don't need expensive lawyers or consultants to get started.
